    LLoyalty

    Loyalty to the Nation

    As land component of the Indian Armed Forces , We uphold the ideals of the Constitution of India, thwart proxy wars and internal threats, assist our Government and people of India.

    FFuture

    Future Ready Army

    A Nation maintains its Armed Forces for strategic deterrence and if compelled; to win wars. We remain ready for full spectrum of challenges and conflicts

    OOrganisation

    Organisation Comes First

    Leaders view their role as promoting the effectiveness of their command and achieving organisational goals.

    TTrained

    Well Trained

    Our dynamic, comprehensive and operationally focussed training addresses the complete spectrum of conflicts and challenges.

    VVictorious

    Always Victorious

    We have a glorious history of valour, sacrifice and victories.

    RResponsibility

    Unlimited Responsibility

    We carry out duties and tasks professionally and without regard to personal safety. We are ever willing to make the supreme sacrifice, if the situation so demands.
